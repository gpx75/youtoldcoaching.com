## What’s Your Leadership Story?

A 45-minute clarity session to reconnect with who you are — and who you are becoming.

Leaders at all levels — emerging, mid-level, senior, executive — rarely get the space to pause and reflect on:

- Who they are becoming
- What their leadership story has shaped in them
- The pressures they have been carrying
- The patterns that hold them back
- The moments where imposter feelings quietly show up
- The confidence they want to step into
- The voice they want to lead with
- The impact they want to make
- The parts of themselves they have had to quieten
- The values they want to honour
- The leader they are ready to grow into

These questions sit beneath the surface, unspoken but deeply felt.

This session gives you a moment to breathe, to be honest, and to reconnect with yourself.

When you leave, you walk away with:

- Clarity about your leadership identity
- Direction on what needs to shift
- Insight into your inner narrative
- Awareness of your strengths and blind spots
- Confidence in your next step
- A personalised recommendation for your leadership journey

This is your starting point. Your moment of truth. Your invitation to step into the leader your story has been waiting for.
