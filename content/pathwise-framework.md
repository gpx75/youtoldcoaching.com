The Pathwise™ Methodology is the proprietary framework at the heart of every engagement with Moya James. It maps the three sequential phases of a meaningful leadership transition.

**Activate** — Start the journey, gain clarity, and take first steps.

You caaaannot move purposefully towards something you haven't yet articulated. Activate is where we excavate your values, your non-negotiables, and the narrative threads that define your leadership distinctly. What you want. Why it matters. What has been holding the story in place.

**Align** — Build capability, connect skills, values, and purpose.

Clarity without capability is aspiration without traction. Align is where we identify the gaps — skills to develop, strengths to name, and the leadership identity to inhabit with full conviction. The work here is both practical and deeply personal.

**Achieve** — Take confident action, create transformation, and deliver results.

Capability without confidence remains potential. Achieve is where the internal work translates into external impact — in how you lead, how you communicate, and how others experience you. This is where transformation becomes visible.

---

The Pathwise™ methodology is narrative-centred. Every tool, every session, every intervention is grounded in the conviction that **the story you tell about your transition shapes the leader you become on the other side of it.**

Narrative coaching is not therapy. It is a precision instrument for articulating leadership identity — making explicit what experience has already taught you, so you can move forward with it deliberately.
