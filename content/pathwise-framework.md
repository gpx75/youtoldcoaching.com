The Transition Triad maps three interlocking deficits that appear in different proportions in every client I work with.

**Clarity** — you know you want something different, but cannot yet articulate what or why. Narrative work excavates this: your values, your non-negotiables, the moments in your career that felt most alive.

**Capability** — sometimes the gap is a skill. More often it is an untold strength — something you have done for years but never named or claimed.

**Confidence** — not bravado. Grounded self-belief that comes from knowing your story and trusting that it is enough. This is the last piece to arrive and the first thing others notice when it does.

The Triad is not a sequential process. Which pillar leads depends on what your transition requires.
